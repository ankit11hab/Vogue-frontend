    Rulebooklet

    THEME
    The theme for prelims is Seven Deadly Sins. Use your creativity and imagination and choose your favourite sin and let your inner devil take over. The final round would be an open theme round.
    TEAM COMPOSITION
    Only 1 choreographer, make-up artist and maximum of 4 designer will be accepted from one team. The minimum age limit for models is 16 years.
    LIGHT & SOUND
    The teams are responsible for their own music and sequence. Music is to be submitted beforehand. If lighting facility is to be availed, one person of the group can supervise the lighting.
    REGISTRATIONS
    Every team has to submit a design of dress of their any previous performance or they can also submit the link to the performance while registering for the event at www.alcheringa.in/registrations.
    TIME LIMIT
    Every team will be given 5 minutes for each round. On exceeding the time limit, negative points will be awarded..
    REWARDS
    Win attractive prizes worth more than Rs 1,12,000. Special Rewards will be given for Best Model, Best Choreographer, Best Makeup and Best Designer.

    HAUTE COUTURE

    REWARDS

    STATE OF DRESSES

    Rulebooklet

    Rulebooklet

    VENUE
    Every team will be given 5 different locations in our campus to do photoshoot of models (duet photoshoot).
    TEAM COMPOSITION
    Every Team has to have 2 models (1 male and 1 female), 2 designers and 1 photographer. Minimum age limit for models is 17 years.
    REGISTRATIONS
    Every team has to submit a design of dress of their any previous performance. The entries are submitted while registering for the event at ww.alcheringa.in/registrations.
    JUDGING CRITERIA
    The teams will be judged upon Creativity / Innovation in design, Standard of Design, Originality, Uniqueness, Product quality, finish and Photography
    TIME
    The Event will be on Day 2 of Alcheringa and the time will be from 10am-5pm. There is no theme to the event.
    REWARDS
    Win attractive prizes worth more than Rs.35,000. Special Rewards will be given for the Best Model and Best Photographer.
